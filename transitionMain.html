The year: 2377. In the aftermath of World War V, the digital landscape lies in ruins.
Cryptography: a lost art.
The world's secrets: exposed.
Chaos reigns as data breaches threaten the very fabric of society.

Agent, your mission is critical. As an operative of the Temporal Intelligence Agency, you must traverse the annals of time to recover lost cryptographic knowledge. The future of data security rests in your hands.

For each temporal incursion, you will receive a classified briefing. Your objective: assist key cryptographers of the past and extract vital cipher intelligence. The identities of your contacts will be revealed in your mission parameters. Remember, history itself hangs in the balance. Proceed with utmost discretion.
