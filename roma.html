Advanced Tips

Frequency Analysis (Cryptanalysis)

Look for the letters which appear most frequently in the ciphertext.

1

Identify common letters in English: E, T, A, O, I, N

2

Count letter frequencies in your ciphertext

3

Compare ciphertext frequencies with known English frequencies

Example:

If 'G' is most common in your ciphertext, it might represent 'E'

G → E

This suggests a +2 shift in the Caesar cipher

Decrypted Message:

3
